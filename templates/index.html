Game Blog is a social game chat app.

Users have the ability to search their favorite games, add them to their profile, and chat about them with whoever else is online.

Our app is unique in the sense that it provides a social game experience without ads or a great amount of overhead, something a lot of other apps implement.

Created by: Nathan Heckman, Ba Choi, Christopher English, Aaron Reyes, Yashesh Patel
