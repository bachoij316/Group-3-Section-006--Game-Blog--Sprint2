Game Blog


Welcome to our app! Using this app you can search for video games that you like, save them and remove them. You can also get the top 5 news items about a topic in gaming. There is a chat feature for you to interact with other people who are in the chat room to discuss your love for video games!

This app matters because you can use it as a jumping off point to find new games and interact with others.

This was made by Nathan Heckman, Ba Choi, Yashesh Patel, Chris English, and Aaron Reyes
